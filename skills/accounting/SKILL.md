# Accounting Skills

- `accounting/audit-support` — Support SOX 404 compliance with control testing methodology, sample selection, and documentation standards.
- `accounting/close-management` — Manage the month-end close process with task sequencing, dependencies, and status tracking.
- `accounting/financial-statements` — Generate income statements, balance sheets, and cash flow statements with GAAP presentation and period-over-period comparison.
- `accounting/journal-entry-prep` — Prepare journal entries with proper debits, credits, and supporting documentation for month-end close.
- `accounting/reconciliation` — Reconcile accounts by comparing GL balances to subledgers, bank statements, or third-party data.
- `accounting/variance-analysis` — Decompose financial variances into drivers with narrative explanations and waterfall analysis.

To load a skill: load_skill(name="accounting/<skill-name>")
