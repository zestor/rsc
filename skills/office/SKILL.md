# Office Skills

- `office/docx` — Create, read, edit, or manipulate Word documents (.docx files) with formatting, tables of contents, headings, and page numbers.
- `office/pdf` — Read, extract, combine, split, rotate, watermark, create, fill forms, encrypt/decrypt, and OCR PDF files.
- `office/pptx` — Create, read, edit, combine, or split PowerPoint presentations (.pptx files) including templates, layouts, and speaker notes.
- `office/theme-factory` — Style non-web artifacts (slides, docs, reports) with pre-set or custom themes including colors and fonts.
- `office/xlsx` — Create, read, edit, or manipulate spreadsheet files (.xlsx, .xlsm, .csv, .tsv) with formulas, formatting, and charting.

To load a skill: load_skill(name="office/<skill-name>")
