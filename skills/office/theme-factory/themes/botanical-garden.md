# Botanical Garden

A fresh and organic theme featuring garden-inspired tones for lively presentations.

## Color Palette

- **Fern Green**: `#4a7c59` - Primary accent (use sparingly — headings, key data, icons)
- **Cream**: `#f5f3ed` - Soft neutral backgrounds
- **Warm Sand**: `#e0d5c4` - Secondary surface
- **Charcoal**: `#2d2d2d` - Text and dark anchors

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Serif Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans

## Best Used For

Garden centers, food presentations, farm-to-table content, botanical brands, natural products.
