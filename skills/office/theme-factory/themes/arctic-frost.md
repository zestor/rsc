# Arctic Frost

A cool and crisp winter-inspired theme that conveys clarity, precision, and professionalism.

## Color Palette

- **Steel Blue**: `#4a6fa5` - Primary accent (use sparingly — headings, key data, dividers)
- **Ice Blue**: `#d4e4f7` - Light neutral backgrounds
- **Silver**: `#c0c0c0` - Neutral borders and subtle dividers
- **Crisp White**: `#fafafa` - Clean backgrounds and body text areas

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Sans Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans

## Best Used For

Healthcare presentations, technology solutions, winter sports, clean tech, pharmaceutical content.
