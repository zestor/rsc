# Desert Rose

A soft and sophisticated theme with dusty, muted tones perfect for elegant presentations.

## Color Palette

- **Deep Burgundy**: `#5d2e46` - Primary accent (use sparingly — headings, key data, dividers)
- **Sand**: `#e8d5c4` - Warm neutral backgrounds
- **Dusty Rose**: `#d4a5a5` - Soft neutral surface tone
- **Clay**: `#b87d6d` - Muted neutral for borders and subtle elements

## Typography

- **Headers**: FreeSans Bold
- **Body Text**: FreeSans

## Best Used For

Fashion presentations, beauty brands, wedding planning, interior design, boutique businesses.
