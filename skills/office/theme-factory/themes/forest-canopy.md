# Forest Canopy

A natural and grounded theme featuring earth tones inspired by dense forest environments.

## Color Palette

- **Forest Green**: `#2d4a2b` - Primary accent (use sparingly — headings, key data, dividers)
- **Sage**: `#7d8471` - Muted neutral for body text and secondary elements
- **Olive**: `#a4ac86` - Soft neutral surface tone
- **Ivory**: `#faf9f6` - Clean backgrounds and body text areas

## Typography

- **Headers**: FreeSerif Bold
- **Body Text**: FreeSans

## Best Used For

Environmental presentations, sustainability reports, outdoor brands, wellness content, organic products.
