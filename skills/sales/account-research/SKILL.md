# Account Research

Research any company or person and produce actionable sales intel.

## Execution Flow

1. Parse target (company name, domain, or person + company)
2. Web search: company overview, recent news, funding, careers, leadership
3. Enrichment (if connected): verified contacts, tech stack, org chart
4. CRM (if connected): prior relationship, past opportunities, activity history
5. Synthesize into a research brief

## Connector Enrichment

| Connector      | What It Adds                                                      |
| -------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Enrichment** | Verified emails, phone, tech stack, org chart, funding details    |
| **CRM**        | Prior relationship history, past opportunities, existing contacts |

## Research Checklist

Prioritize enrichment data over web (more accurate) when both are available.

- Company basics: what they do, size, industry, HQ, funding stage
- Recent news (last 90 days): funding rounds, leadership changes, product launches
- Hiring signals: open roles by department, growth velocity
- Key people: name, title, tenure, background, talking points
- Tech stack: cloud, data, CRM, relevant tooling
- Qualification signals: positive indicators, concerns, unknowns for discovery

## Output Sections

1. **Quick Take** -- 2-3 sentences on who they are, why they might need you, best outreach angle
2. **Company Profile** -- firmographic table
3. **Key People** -- per-person card with background and talking points
4. **Qualification Signals** -- positive, concerns, unknowns
5. **Recommended Approach** -- best entry point, opening hook, discovery questions

## Gotchas

- **Enrichment data can be stale** -- employee counts and org charts lag by 3-6 months; cross-reference with LinkedIn.
- **Domain-based lookup is more precise** -- "research acme.com" avoids ambiguity vs. common company names.
- **Hiring velocity matters more than headcount** -- 20 open ML roles signals strategy shifts that raw size does not.
- **CRM contacts may be outdated** -- people change roles; always verify titles before outreach.
- **Don't conflate research with qualification** -- this skill gathers facts; use discovery calls to validate assumptions.
