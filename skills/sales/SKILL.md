# Sales Skills

- `sales/account-research` — Research a company or person and get actionable sales intel with web search and enrichment tools.
- `sales/call-prep` — Prepare for a sales call with account context, attendee research, and suggested agenda.
- `sales/competitive-intelligence` — Research competitors and build an interactive battlecard with comparison matrix.
- `sales/create-an-asset` — Generate tailored sales assets (landing pages, decks, one-pagers, workflow demos) from deal context.
- `sales/daily-briefing` — Start your day with a prioritized sales briefing from calendar, CRM, and email.
- `sales/draft-outreach` — Research a prospect then draft personalized outreach using web research and CRM data.

To load a skill: load_skill(name="sales/<skill-name>")
