# Professional Documents and Assets

Computer produces finished, shareable deliverables — not drafts or text dumps.

## Document formats

| Format   | Capabilities                                                                                                                                     |
| -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **PDF**  | Create from scratch, merge multiple PDFs, split pages, rotate, add watermarks, fill form fields, encrypt/password-protect, OCR scanned documents |
| **DOCX** | Word documents with table of contents, styled headings, page numbers, letterheads, custom formatting                                             |
| **PPTX** | Slide decks with brand-consistent layouts, speaker notes, charts, and data visualizations                                                        |
| **XLSX** | Spreadsheets with formulas, conditional formatting, charts, and multi-sheet workbooks                                                            |

## Media generation

**AI images** — Generate photorealistic photos, illustrations, diagrams, and artistic images from text descriptions or reference images already in the workspace, including uploaded files, searched images, screenshots, and prior generated outputs. Useful for presentations, websites, social media, and creative projects.

**AI video** — Generate short video clips from text descriptions or animate a starting/reference image already in the workspace, including uploaded files, searched images, screenshots, and prior generated outputs. Supports landscape and portrait orientations.

**Video editing** — Edit uploaded or online-accessible videos: trim and splice clips, overlay audio tracks, adjust aspect ratios (e.g., landscape to vertical for mobile), burn in live subtitles, and control playback speed.

**Text-to-speech** — Convert text to natural-sounding audio with multiple AI voices. Supports single-speaker narration and multi-speaker dialogue.

**Transcription** — Transcribe audio and video files with speaker diarization (identifies who said what, up to 32 speakers), word-level timestamps, and support for 99 languages.

Image and video generation support model selection for different quality and cost profiles. Consult the model-catalog skill for available options.

## Quality assurance

Every asset is created in the workspace, visually inspected for layout issues (text wrapping, overflow, alignment), and delivered as a downloadable file. Computer catches and fixes formatting problems before sharing.

## Example use cases

- Generate a branded slide deck from research findings.
- Create a PDF report with charts, tables, and inline citations.
- Build a multi-sheet Excel workbook from raw data with formulas and formatting.
- Transcribe a meeting recording with speaker labels and timestamps.
- Generate product images or illustrations for a presentation.
