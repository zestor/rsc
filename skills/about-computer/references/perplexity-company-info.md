# Perplexity — Company Overview

Computer is built by Perplexity. What started as an answer engine — replacing search results with direct, cited answers — has grown into a multi-product AI platform spanning search, agents, a browser, developer APIs, and more.

## What Perplexity builds

Perplexity's products share a common thread: grounding AI in real, verifiable information. Every answer cites its sources so users can trust and verify what they read.

- **Perplexity Search** — The flagship answer engine. Users ask questions in natural language and get direct, cited answers synthesized from the web, academic sources, and more — not a page of blue links.
- **Perplexity Pro** — A subscription at $20/month with access to advanced models, higher usage limits, and deeper research and analysis capabilities.
- **Perplexity Max** — The most advanced tier, for power users who need the highest-volume access. Includes Perplexity's most advanced product features and frontier models, early access to new products, the highest agentic query limits on Comet and Computer, and priority support.
- **Perplexity Computer** — An AI agent that goes beyond answering questions to doing work: research, code execution, document creation, browser automation, 400+ app integrations, persistent memory, and scheduling — all in a single conversational session.
- **Comet** — An AI-native web browser built on Chromium. Summarizes pages, compares tabs, automates workflows, and integrates with hundreds of apps.
- **API Platform** — Developer access to Perplexity's search and retrieval infrastructure for building AI-powered applications and agents.

## Founding story

Perplexity was founded in August 2022 by a team with deep roots in AI research and engineering. The company was built around the insight that search should deliver answers, not just links.
